Our journey

Luke, Craig and Nic first connected in the early 1990s during their training with the Queensland Master Builders Association (QMBA). Craig and Nic, however, share an even deeper connection that traces its roots back to the late 70s, where they first met ironically building sandcastles together at kindergarten, cementing a mateship that has endured for over four decades.

Throughout their individual journeys with some of Australia’s largest builders and suppliers, they honed their skills and grew, each treading unique paths. Then, in 2006, a moment of destiny unfolded as they joined forces and evermore+ was born. Today, Luke, Craig, and Nic each play a vital role in delivering exquisitely evermore+ designed homes with unique and distinctive features, inclusive pricing, and quality craftsmanship.

Nearly two decades ago, a small team of property experts laid the foundation for what would become a respected building firm, celebrated for its exceptional custom projects throughout South East Queensland.

Our journey began with a vision to provide ‘more+’ – more style, more choice, more care, delivering quality homes that authentically capture the dreams of our clients.

With a passion to serve discerning clients dissatisfied with a cookie-cutter approach, evermore+ set forth on a mission to lead the industry in tailored design and construction. This commitment positioned evermore+ as a trusted partner, guiding projects from initial concept and interior design to final construction stage.

This approach has allowed us to uphold excellence in numerous evermore+ designed homes throughout the region

Our belief

At evermore+ we believe that bespoke design and quality construction are essential for discerning home buyers who seek to move beyond the limitations of project home builders.

With over 300 homes crafted in the past two decades, we stand as your ideal project partner, adept at striking a balance between design, interiors, and construction, ensuring the flawless delivery of your dream evermore+ home.

Our genuine commitment to this belief ensures that evermore+ delivers more+ style, more+ choice, with more+ care
