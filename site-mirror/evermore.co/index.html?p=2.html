Delivering bespoke homes

with a personal touch

From our humble beginnings, evermore+ homes continues to grow and solidify our name as one of Queensland’s leading builders. Our services are designed to
celebrate our clients, which is why we offer a comprehensive range of options to suit every budget and vision.

Our full-scale in-house team offers our clients a streamlined experience, fast-tracks the process and delivers genuinely unique
homes. While we pride ourselves on our exemplary end-to-end offering, our custom approach  gives us the flexibility to tailor our work around the individual
needs of every project and client.

DESIGN AND CONSTRUCT
The evermore+ design and construction process takes clients from design concept to completion. Our esteemed team of in-house Architect, Project Manager, Interior designers, and skilled Tradespeople guarantee that every stage is executed according to your unique preferences, all while maintaining budget integrity, exceptional craftsmanship, and admired lasting design.

Whether you have a vacant block, a knock down rebuild site, or you require a major renovation, the evermore+ approach ensures every client will enjoy the flexibility of a tailored experience and unwavering results.
KNOCK DOWN REBUILD
Do you cherish your existing location but require more space for your growing family? Our expert team specializes in the knockdown and rebuild journey.

From initial site analysis and opportunity assessment to demolition and the crafting of your new custom designed home, evermore+ is the pathway to turning your dream into reality, right in your beloved neighbourhood.
SLOPING AND DIFFICULT SITES
Our highly competent team is always prepared to take on challenging projects, making us skilled in the handling of small lots with narrow frontages to sloping and difficult sites. There is a solution for every challenge that a site may throw at us, and this guiding principle underpins every project we design and construct, regardless of the site’s complexity.

When you partner with evermore+, you’re tapping into a wealth of renowned design and building expertise.
HOME RENOVATIONS
At evermore+, we understand the sentimental value of certain parts of your existing home, and we recognize that local council regulations can sometimes limit the feasibility of complete knockdown projects. This is where major renovations come into play, rekindling the essence of any home and its unique location. Our team of skilled professionals is here to join forces with you, providing collaborative design and construction to bring your dream renovation to life.

The evermore+ team are renowned experts in existing property transformations through custom home renovations and extensions. It’s the ideal way to capitalise on your investment to create the home of your dreams without having to give up the location you already love.
HAMPTON STYLE HOMES
For nearly two decades, evermore+ has specialized in creating elegant Hamptons-inspired homes. Our custom designs blend coastal sophistication with a perfect balance of luxury and warmth, ideal for South East Queensland’s relaxed lifestyle.

evermore+ has successfully delivered many Hamptons-style projects, delivering a timeless “forevermore+” home that radiates elegance & beauty.
PROPERTY DEVELOPERS
Collaborating closely with property developers, evermore+ leverages over a decade of architectural design expertise to create exceptional residential and commercial developments. When you partner with evermore+ our teamwork within project feasibilities to ensure that developments destined for sale or rental leave a lasting impression.

The evermore+ team have successfully delivered numerous projects for property developers. Our end-to-end services streamline the design and build process.

Connect with evermore+ to add a unique touch to your next development lineup.
