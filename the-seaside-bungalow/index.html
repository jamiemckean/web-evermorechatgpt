The Seaside
Bungalow

  • Style - California Bungalow
  • Bed Room - 4
  • Bathroom - 4
  • Carspace - 2

Framed by bay views and carefully landscaped gardens, this home reinterprets the classic California bungalow through a contemporary coastal lens. Every detail has been thoughtfully considered to combine timeless character with modern functionality, creating a refined and enduring residence.

Set on a generous 478m² corner block, the design balances architectural precision with warmth and liveability. French oak floors, Italian terrazzo tiles and raked ceilings up to 4.5 m create a sense of openness, while a soft coastal palette maximises natural light and captures the surrounding views. At the centre, the kitchen is a functional and visual focal point, featuring a natural stone island, Miele appliances, integrated Liebherr fridge-freezer, Tupac shaker cabinetry and a walk-through pantry. The adjoining living and dining areas flow seamlessly to a sunlit alfresco deck, creating a strong connection between indoor and outdoor spaces.

The home accommodates family life with four bedrooms and four bathrooms, including a master suite with a walk-through dressing room and luxe ensuite. The lounge, anchored by a gas fireplace, opens to leafy park vistas.

Crafted with care, the home incorporates indoor and outdoor fireplaces, irrigation, full Crimsafe security, custom timber joinery and wool carpets, all reflecting evermore’s commitment to quality finishes, durability and considered design.

A modern coastal home that balances enduring charm with contemporary design, creating a landmark residence in Manly. Skilled craftsmanship and refined detailing define this Bayside property, where classic influences meet modern living
